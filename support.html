ShiftPulse: Support

Getting Started

ShiftPulse is a wellness and financial planning app built for shift workers. It helps you track fatigue, manage your schedule, estimate your pay, and monitor hydration.

Key Features

  • Fatigue Score: An informational estimate of your alertness based on sleep data from Apple Health
  • Schedule Management: Set up repeating shift patterns or add individual shifts
  • Pay Estimation: Configure your base wage, differentials, and see gross/net estimates
  • Hydration Tracking: Log water intake with reminders on your Apple Watch
  • Retirement Planning: Track contribution projections based on your earnings
  • Insights: View trends in fatigue, sleep, hours worked, and earnings over time

Apple Watch

ShiftPulse includes an Apple Watch companion app with complications for fatigue score, heart rate, and step count. The watch app syncs automatically with your iPhone.

Important Disclaimer

ShiftPulse is not a medical device. The Fatigue Score and Alertness Check features provide estimated wellness metrics for informational purposes only. They have not been clinically validated. Never rely solely on this app to determine if you are fit for duty, safe to drive, or safe to operate machinery. Always trust your own judgment and consult a healthcare professional with any medical concerns.
