Privacy Policy

ShiftPulse

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Summary

ShiftPulse respects your privacy. All your data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information.

Data Collection

ShiftPulse does not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No location tracking
  • No usage analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No cookies or identifiers
  • No data is transmitted to any server
  • No advertisements
  • No data shared with third parties

Local Data

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Shift schedules and patterns
  • Pay configuration (hourly wage, differentials, holidays)
  • Retirement contribution settings
  • Hydration logs
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Apple Health (HealthKit)

With your explicit permission, ShiftPulse reads the following from Apple Health:

  • Sleep analysis data (to calculate the Fatigue Score)
  • Heart rate data (for wellness features)

ShiftPulse does not write data to Apple Health. ShiftPulse does not share HealthKit data with anyone, including the developer.

Data Deletion

If you delete ShiftPulse from your device, all locally stored data is permanently deleted. There is no cloud backup or recovery.

Children's Privacy

ShiftPulse is intended for adults aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

If we update this policy, changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
